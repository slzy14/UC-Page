Innovation and Technology Transfer Office

The University of the Cordilleras Innovation and Technology Transfer Office (UC INTO) was established under the Intellectual Property Management Program for Academic Institutions Commercializing Technologies ( IMPACT) Program of the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) in November of 2020. The University was screened to undergo training under the United States Agency International Development- Science, Technology, Research and Innovation for Development (USAID- STRIDE) Program. The USAID STRIDE program's goal is to strengthen the science, technology, research, and innovation capacity in Philippine higher education, focusing on disciplines that contribute to high-growth economic sectors. With this, the grant has been conferred to the University of the Cordilleras, one of the top 10 qualifiers for the IMPACT Program.

The UC-INTO, under the Vice President for Academics and Research, is responsible for the implementation of the IP and the Tech Transfer Policies of the University through assisting the employees, students, and partners in commercializing technology created and developed at UC. In doing so, the INTO strives to contribute to the public good, further economic development in the region, and enhance the research and development reputation of the University.

Vision-Mission

Vision
To be the country's leading university-based center in creating, protecting, and providing innovative solutions toward national development.

Mission
To build a community for innovative solutions by providing strong support in the pursuit of ideation, creation, protection, and commercialization of innovative works to improve society's quality of life.

Core Values

The UC Innovation and Technology Transfer Office seeks to serve its community by incorporating the following core values in delivering its commitment:

Innovation:

We embrace and respond to the constantly changing needs of the society

Motivation:

We are driven to encourage the disclosure and protection of technology to improve society's quality of life, resulting in national development.

Professionalism:

We commit to exercise responsibility, integrity, excellence, and accountability in everything we do.

Adaptability:

We promote nation-building by responding to the changing demands and problems of the community.

Collaboration:

We believe that partnership and collaboration will drive the efficient and effective creation of solutions that will solve society's problems.

Trust:

We foster trust in all our endeavors to establish our relationship with our stakeholders.
