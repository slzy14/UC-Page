Career Education Program

Resume Writing

A webinar / infographic tool that assists graduating students in crafting their resume and / or portfolios as they get ready for their career transition.

Virtual Interview Guide

Career Assistance Program

Resume Review

A service where students are encouraged to submit their resumes for critiquing and improvement.

Virtual Mock Interview

A service where students can experience how virtual interviews work and gain tips on how one can improve with the help of trained staff.

Career Advising

Provision of career advising service to students who are unsure of their career path.

Partner industries share their industry life and practices where students can gain insights about their career path.

Sharing of industry job ads and career opportunity opening for graduating students and alumni.
